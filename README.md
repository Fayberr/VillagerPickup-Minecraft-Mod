# 🧑‍🌾 Villager Pickup

Moving Villagers doesn't have to be a struggle. No more boats, minecarts, or frustrating pathfinding. **Villager Pickup** lets you pick up any Villager and move them with ease!

Simply **Shift + Right-Click** a Villager to turn them into a Spawn Egg.

---

### ✨ Features
*   **📦 Easy Moving:** Pick up villagers and place them back down exactly where you want them.
*   **📜 Detailed Tooltips:** Hover over the Spawn Egg to see everything about your Villager:
    *   **Profession & Level:** See their job and how high their level is.
    *   **Workstation & Bed:** Shows the coordinates of where they are linked.
    *   **Trade List:** View their trades directly in the item description.
    *   **Enchanted Books:** If a Librarian has an Enchanted Book trade, it will show the **exact enchantment and level** (e.g., Mending I) right in the tooltip!
*   **🍦 Purely Server-Side:** This mod only needs to be on the server. Friends can join with a completely Vanilla Minecraft client!
*   **✅ Data-Safe:** Saves all trades, XP, and discounts. Nothing is lost when you pick them up.

---

### 🚀 How to Use
1.  **Pick Up:** Crouch (Shift) and Right-Click a Villager with an empty hand.
2.  **Move:** The Villager is now an item in your inventory.
3.  **Place:** Use the Spawn Egg on the ground to put your Villager back.
